Some tips on playing through the game to reach the end screen: 
- There are a total of 4 street lamps. 
- Revisit the shop to buy items. 
- Fireflies respawn in maps. 
- Interact with citizens for tips and information about the town. 
- The buttons 1, 2, 3 and are used for buying and using items.
